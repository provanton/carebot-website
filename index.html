Motivations

  • Leading patients to the best, most efficient healthcare options
    • Providence offers ExpressCare/VirtualCare to accommodate lesser illnesses.
    • However, not all patients are aware of ExpressCare/VirtualCare, or what symptoms are addressed by those options.
    • They may end up going to a primary care physician or the emergency room, when it's unnecessary.
    • CareBot can lead patients to ExpressCare/VirtualCare by symptom analysis.
  • Ease of use
    • CareBot aims to be a "perfect assistant."
      • Takes care of logistics with minimal guidance.
      • Just tell it your symptoms/what you want, and it will handle the rest.
    • Accessible
      • No app/website required.
      • Available through: SMS, Skype, Slack -- like an actual assistant.
    • Easy to talk with
      • Natural Language Processing with LUIS
      • Rich Visual/Interactive media (hero cards, carousels, etc.)
